================================================================================
[GAME IDEA]
================================================================================
Title: "Void Survivor"
Genre: Top-down Arena Survival Shooter
Engine: Love2D 11.5

Concept:
- Player pilots a spaceship/character in a 2D arena inspired by dark sci-fi ruins and space-battle scenery
- Waves of enemies spawn from the edges of the screen and escalate in number, pressure, and danger
- The main goal is to survive as long as possible, chase high scores, and build short-session replayability
- Power-ups dropped by enemies create moment-to-moment build variety and reward aggressive play
- The game aims for simple but addictive arcade-style gameplay with strong neon visual feedback and readable combat pacing

================================================================================
[GAME DESIGN]
================================================================================

--- PLAYER ---
- Moves with WASD or Arrow keys
- Aims with mouse
- Shoots with left mouse button (auto-fire option)
- Has health bar (starts with 100 HP)
- Speed: moderate, responsive controls
- Invincibility frames after taking damage (0.5s)

--- ENEMIES ---
Type 1 - Chaser (Red): Moves directly toward player, low HP
Type 2 - Shooter (Purple): Keeps distance, shoots projectiles at player
Type 3 - Tank (Orange): Slow, high HP, large size, high damage on contact
Type 4 - Speeder (Yellow): Very fast, low HP, appears in later waves

--- WAVES ---
- Wave 1-3: Only Chasers
- Wave 4-6: Chasers + Shooters
- Wave 7-9: Chasers + Shooters + Tanks
- Wave 10+: All types, increasing quantity
- 5 second break between waves
- Enemy count = base_count + (wave * 2)

--- POWER-UPS ---
- Health Pack (Green +): Restores 25 HP
- Speed Boost (Blue): +50% speed for 5 seconds
- Rapid Fire (Yellow): 2x fire rate for 5 seconds
- Shield (Cyan): Blocks next hit

--- SCORING ---
- Chaser kill: 10 points
- Shooter kill: 20 points
- Tank kill: 30 points
- Speeder kill: 15 points
- Wave completion bonus: wave_number * 50

--- SCREENS ---
1. Title Screen: Game title, difficulty selection, high score display, quick controls hint
2. Gameplay Screen: Main combat view with HUD and active power-up readouts
3. Pause Screen: "PAUSED" overlay with resume / restart / quit-to-menu actions
4. Settings Screen: target menu for audio, screen shake, auto-fire, and future remap options
5. Game Over Screen: final score, kill breakdown, restart and return-to-menu flow

--- HUD ---
- Top-left: HP bar
- Top-right: Score and Best score
- Top-center: Wave number and combo feedback
- Bottom-center: Active power-up indicators and weapon timer
- Visual direction: futuristic circular / sci-fi motifs while keeping the HUD readable during heavy combat

--- ARENA ---
- Fixed size: 800x600 (window size)
- Dark background with subtle grid pattern and parallax stars
- Background art direction can evolve toward alien ruins / shipwreck silhouettes from the concept references
- Borders are walls (player and enemies confined)

--- CONTROLS & ACCESSIBILITY ---
- Primary input: WASD / Arrows for movement + Mouse for aim/shoot
- Current quick actions: ESC pause, Enter restart/start, F11 fullscreen
- Target support: gamepad input, remappable controls, music/SFX sliders, screen shake toggle
- UI should keep high contrast, readable text, and color + icon based status indicators

--- ART & AUDIO DIRECTION ---
- Player ship direction: sleek, fast, heroic silhouette; warm orange/yellow paint is a strong candidate
- Enemy presentation should contrast clearly with the player using bold threat colors and readable silhouettes
- Audio direction: retro synth / electronic / space ambient
- Effects should emphasize neon hits, explosions, boss phase changes, and arcade intensity

--- QA TARGETS ---
- Maintain smooth 60 FPS on target desktop hardware
- Track playtest metrics: average survival time, average wave reached, perceived fairness, and HUD readability

Current File Structure:
  Project/
    main.lua                              -- Thin Love2D entry point
    conf.lua                              -- Love2D configuration
    controllers/
      GameController.lua                  -- Main game orchestration
      StateController.lua                 -- Menu / play / pause / gameover
      input/InputController.lua           -- Keyboard + mouse input
      audio/AudioController.lua           -- Procedural 8-bit SFX
      data/SaveController.lua             -- Save / load high score + settings
      gameplay/EntityController.lua       -- Player / enemies / bullets / powerups
      gameplay/CollisionController.lua    -- Combat + pickup collisions
      gameplay/SpawnController.lua        -- Difficulty-aware spawning
      gameplay/WaveController.lua         -- Wave pacing + boss waves
      gameplay/ScoreController.lua        -- Score + combo handling
      rendering/BackgroundRenderer.lua    -- Grid + star parallax
      rendering/UIRenderer.lua            -- HUD + menu + overlay rendering
      rendering/RenderController.lua      -- Render composition + camera shake
    entities/
      Player.lua                          -- Player entity + weapon power-ups
      Bullet.lua                          -- Player / enemy / heavy bullets
      PowerUp.lua                         -- Health / speed / rapid / shield / weapons
      enemies/                            -- Chaser / Shooter / Tank / Speeder / Boss
    systems/
      EventBus.lua                        -- Pub/sub system
      Particles.lua                       -- Global particle effects
    utils/
      Constants.lua                       -- Tunable constants
